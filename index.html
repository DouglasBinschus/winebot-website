🍷 Wine Recommendation Chatbot

You can include these factors in your prompt:

Country • Province • Region • Variety • Winery • Designation • Points • Price • Tasting notes

Try prompts like:

  • “Recommend a top Italian wine with cherry tasting notes.”
  • “Find a wine from California under $30 with red fruit notes.”
  • “Show wines by winery ‘X’ with 90+ points.”
  • “Suggest a Pinot Noir from Oregon with earthy notes.”

Tip: You can combine multiple constraints (e.g., country + price + points + tasting notes).
